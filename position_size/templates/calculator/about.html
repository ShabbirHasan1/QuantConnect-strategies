Simple web calculator that returns the correct position size when buying stock shares.


  • Choose the currency value of your account.
  • Type in the size of your account (Equity).
  • Risk ratio rappresents the risk that you are willing to take per trade calculated in % of the total amount.
  • Stop loss in $ is how many dollars below the initial price you plan to close the trade in case of loss.
  • "Stocks" gives you the option to choose from a dropdown menu list the stock you are interested in.
  • After pressing "Calculate" you will get the amount of shares you need to buy in order to risk the amount you planed.
